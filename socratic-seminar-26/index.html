Details

Join us for another amazing Bitcoin meetup this coming Sunday! We're going to have free pizza and beer right when the doors open so make sure to get there early in order to network with other local Bitcoiners.

This month we have the pleasure of hosting Steve Lee, Lead at Spiral, who will be sharing what they're building at Spiral and also briefly touching on the Bitcoin Design Community. Spiral (formerly known as Square Crypto) is an offshoot of Square whose focus is to make bitcoin more than an investment. Some of the projects they are developing include Bitcoin Dev Ket, Lightning Dev Kit, and the Bitcoin Design Community.

The talk will then be followed by a Socratic Seminar and then we will get drinks at a local restaurant after.
